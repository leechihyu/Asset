## Government Responsibility and Social Support

### **helppoor** – Government help to improve poor Americans’ living standards
This scale asked whether the federal government in Washington should do everything possible to improve the standard of living of all poor Americans (Point 1) or whether it is not the government’s responsibility and each person should take care of themselves (Point 5).  Respondents selected points 1–5 on the scale or opted for “don’t know”file:///home/oai/share/GSS2024_Ballot2_English.pdf#:~:text=FEHIRE%3A%20Categorical%20,disagree%2C%20or%20strongly%20disagree%20that.

- **4**

- **Refused**
- **Not applicable**
